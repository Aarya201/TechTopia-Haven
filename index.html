Sensor-Based Street Lights

An LDR (Light Dependent Resistor) street light utilizes a light-sensitive sensor to detect ambient light levels. When it detects darkness, the resistor's high resistance triggers the light to automatically illuminate, providing efficient and energy-saving street lighting. During daylight, the low resistance turns the light low, conserving energy.

Smart Wall

A smart wall integrates motion sensors or touch-sensitive technology, activating a light upon detecting hand proximity. Through infrared or capacitive sensors, the wall responds to movement, triggering the light to illuminate when a hand is sensed nearby, offering convenient and responsive lighting solutions within a living or work space.

Charging Table

A charging table incorporates wireless or wired charging technology to power up mobile devices such as phones or tablets. Embedded coils or USB ports enable seamless charging by simply placing the device on the designated surface. This multifunctional table offers convenient and efficient charging capabilities while serving as a functional furniture piece.

Laser Security System

A laser security system employs a laser beam transmitted along a home's perimeter using strategically positioned mirrors. An LDR (Light Dependent Resistor) receives the reflected beam. When the beam is obstructed, signaling a breach, the LDR triggers an alarm or buzzer, alerting of a potential intrusion, ensuring property safety.

Automatic Shower

Indulge in the convenience of a smart home's automatic shower, where your phone becomes the shower maestro. Adjust water temperature and flow remotely via an app, customizing your shower experience. No more chilly surprises—just personalized, relaxing showers at your fingertips.

Bug Zapper Lights

Experience the innovation of UV bug zapper lights—enticing insects with UV light strips, then swiftly neutralizing them with a potent electric beam. Drawing pests in, these lights provide an effective, eco-friendly solution, ensuring a bug-free environment without the hassle of traditional methods.
